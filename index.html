The strongest sorcerers in Jujutsu Kaisen Characters Details Skills
Satoru Gojo Satoru Gojo is one of 4 Special-grade Sorcerers who is the first person to inherit the Limitless and Six eyes after 400 years. He was widely recognized as the strongest sorcer in the Modern Era. Domain Expansion, Reverse Cursed Technique, Hollow Purple
Ryomen Sukuna Sukuna is regarded as the strongest Sorcerer over 1000 years ago, and claimed the title "King of Curses". He is regarded as one of the main antagonists of the Series, and was heavily unstoppable. Domain Expansion, Reversed Cursed Technique, Cleave
Suguru Geto Suguru Geto USED to be one of 4 Special-Grade Sorcerers, until he was banned from Jujutsu High, because killing a hundred non-sorcerers in a night. This eventually made him the worst curse user of all time. Curse Manipulation
Yuta Okkotsu Yuta Okkotsu is also among the Special-Grade Sorcerers, who used to be hunted by the curse of his late childhood friend Rika Orimoto. He was able to adapt the cursed technique of any sorcerer like the Cleave of Sukuna, and the cursed speech. Cleave, Reverse Cursed Technique, Domain Expansion, Cursed Speech
